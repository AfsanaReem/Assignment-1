Donate Your Qurbani 2022

Many needy people wait to taste beef/mutton only on the Eid of Qurbani every year. That is why every year the As-Sunnah Foundation organizes the distribution of meat of Qurbani under the title 'Qurbani for All'. Since Qurbani is an act of worship that can be performed through a representative, the Foundation distributes meat on behalf of the well-to-do and distributes meat among the destitute and helpless people in various poor areas of the country, including North Bengal.
